Granny's Brownies are an unforgrttable classic. Rich, sumputuous belgian chocolate pieces, hidden in a thick, chewy, crunchy explosion of goodness.

Their recipie’s story began more than forty years ago, in the mixing bowl of Cali and her childhood friend Jo. It was Jo’s Granny that guided the first batch, and after decades of practice and honing, Granny’s Brownies now very special indeed.
