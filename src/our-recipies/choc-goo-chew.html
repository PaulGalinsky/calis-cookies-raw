Choc Goo Chew

To bake like Cali...

Begin by preheating your oven to 165 degrees celcius, and liberally buttering three sheets of baking paper.

Take a sieve and airily sift your baking soda and flour into a bowl. Add your pinch of salt and chocolate chips, give one last stir, then set this base aside.

Gently melt your butter, then mix in your sugars. With this done, thoroughly beat your vanilla, egg and yolks into the pan, to make a dreamy goo. Now add your base from the bowl, and stir stir stir.

Lallop your cookie dough onto your baking sheets, giving them a few generous inches to find their independence. At Cali’s we’d make around ten cookies from this mixture.

Bake for around fifteen minutes, allowing a good toasting on the edges, but not too much solidity in the middle. Cool for a few minutes, then indluge.

Ingredients

  • 250g plain flour
  • 3g baking soda
  • 200g unsalted butter, melted
  • 225g packed brown sugar
  • 120g white sugar
  • 15ml vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 300g chocolate chips
  • a pinch of salt
