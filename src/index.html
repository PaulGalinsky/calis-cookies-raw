By the time Cali was nine, she’d learned most of her mum’s biscuit recipies. The kitchen was the beating heart of their traditional Scottish family, and the blueprint for everything that Cali’s Cookies would become.

Cali’s baking, is about finding the purest, most delicious ingredients, and nurturing them into something magnificent, with the perfect combination of time, heat, and just a pinch of naughtiness.

At Cali’s Cookies, baking is an obsession, and we’d love you understand why. Visit us on Broughton Street, Edinburgh for cake and coffee, or place your bespoke order for collection today.
